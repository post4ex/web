Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about our services.

General Questions

Enter your AWB or Reference Number in the tracking widget on the home page. Our real-time tracking system shows the current location and status of your parcel.
Restricted items include explosives, flammable gases, toxic substances, and illegal goods. See our DGR (Dangerous Goods Regulations) section for the full list.
Delays can occur due to weather, customs clearance, or technical issues. If your package is significantly delayed, please raise a complaint so our team can investigate.
Use our Rate Calculator or contact our sales team. Costs are based on weight, dimensions, and destination.

Account & Billing

Go to the Wallet page and use the UPI payment option to top up your balance. Funds are credited after payment confirmation.
Invoices are generated automatically after booking. You can download them from the Billing section in your dashboard or contact support with your AWB number.

Support

You can reach us via email at or raise a ticket through the Complaint page.
Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM – 7 PM IST. For urgent issues, use the complaint portal which is monitored 24/7.
