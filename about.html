Our Story

was built from the ground up to solve a simple problem: Indian logistics businesses deserved better software. Off-the-shelf tools were either too generic or too expensive, and most couldn't handle the complexity of multi-carrier operations, GST compliance, and the sheer variety of shipping documents required.

What started as an internal tool to streamline carrier assignment and rate calculation grew into a full-fledged operations platform. We added real-time tracking via SSE, a comprehensive document suite with 20+ templates, a complete financial module (Vault), and deep carrier integrations — all while keeping the interface fast and familiar.

Today, powers logistics operations across India — from order booking to final delivery — helping businesses focus on what they do best: shipping.
